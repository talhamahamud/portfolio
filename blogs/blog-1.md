The Idea basically came from my beloved *wife*. Her perspective was that if we continue consuming rich food at that high level, it will make us highly self-centered in our desires, which is very bad. **Because uncontrolled humans are not better than animals.** They just thought about them, where they can help other people with their extra wealth who are in a critical situation.
So, the idea is very simple: when we cook some good food, we will share the meal with others. Our perspective was that we would make the process as simple as possible. Because sometimes rigid good processes prevent us from doing good things. Like, "oh, if I gave him/her this quantity of food, what he/she will thought?" or "will she/he like this food?" etc. 

Now, with whom share the foods? Here, our thought is that the people with whom we regularly meet. It could be our friends, the security guards, the van drivers of our University, or the known people of our society. 
Now, as soon as you think, you act. We cooked chicken, then packed it in a simple, beautiful box, and then called one of the well-known van drivers on our campus. We were very nervous, but when he came to our pre-fixed place, we gave it to him and told our perspective. It was a very amazing experience. The expression on his face was **marvelous**. 
## The Benefits
Though this is a very small initiative, we know it has a very deep impact on our society. Humans are social beings. So, the social relations within a society have a very important impact on a human mind, perspective, and since the body and mind are connected, so on the body.

For the past few years, I noticed one thing that **humans love to be treated and gifted very much.** It could be a very small gift that does not cost very much or a small treat, but it makes humans very happy and gives good perspectives about the gifter or the treater.

This type of initiative increases positive perspectives about the people around us. The important benefit is that it helps people do good or take good initiative in society. It motivates those who are captured by their fatigue. Sometimes people want to be good but can't be because of their surroundings. This type of work has a very significant impact on those people. It gives them good perspectives and motivates them to be good and do good.

Lastly, we believed that every good work has some positive impact on this universe. It could change us, the people around us, and the world. On the other hand, it gives us positive energy to survive in this cruel and very hard world.
